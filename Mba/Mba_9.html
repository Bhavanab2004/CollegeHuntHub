PES University — MBA Program

PES University is one of Bangalore’s leading private institutions offering management education with strong corporate connect and research‐oriented learning. Its MBA program emphasizes holistic development, industry exposure, and leadership skills.

Unique Features

  • Accredited university with modern infrastructure and corporate partnerships.
  • Strong industry interface, internships, and live projects with leading firms.
  • Focus on entrepreneurship, technology-driven management, and global business outlook.
  • Active center for research and collaborations with industry and international tie-ups.
