Alliance University — Alliance School of Business, Bengaluru

Alliance University is one of the top private universities in Bengaluru, recognized for its modern campus, academic excellence, and international collaborations. The Alliance School of Business offers a dynamic MBA program designed to shape future business leaders with strong corporate and entrepreneurial skills.

🏫 Key Highlights

  • Modern 55-acre green campus located in Anekal, Bengaluru
  • Top-ranked private business school with global accreditations
  • Strong corporate tie-ups and international exchange programs
  • Dedicated placement and career advancement cell
  • Focus on leadership, innovation, and ethics-driven management

🎓 MBA Program Details

The MBA at Alliance School of Business is a 2-year full-time program offering specializations in Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations, International Business, and Business Analytics.

📘 Fee Structure (2025 Intake)

  • Total Fees: ₹14,00,000 (approx.)
  • Includes tuition, learning materials, and student development programs
  • Hostel and accommodation: ₹1,20,000 – ₹1,80,000 per year (based on facilities)

💼 Placement Highlights

  • Highest Package: ₹26 LPA
  • Average Package: ₹8.5 LPA
  • Top Recruiters: Deloitte, Amazon, KPMG, TCS, HDFC Bank, Infosys, EY
  • Placement Rate: 95%+

📝 Admission Process

  • Accepted Exams: CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, NMAT, GMAT, or Alliance Management Aptitude Test (AMAT)
  • Selection: Essay Writing + Personal Interview
