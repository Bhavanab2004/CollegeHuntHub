RV College of Engineering (RVCE) - MBA

The Department of Management Studies, RV College of Engineering (RVCE), Bengaluru, offers a comprehensive MBA program focused on innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership. The program blends technical and managerial insights, equipping students for careers in business strategy, analytics, finance, and technology management.

Unique Features

  • Industry-integrated curriculum with emphasis on business analytics and technology management.
  • Highly qualified faculty from both academic and corporate backgrounds.
  • Dedicated placement cell with strong connections to IT, finance, and consulting industries.
  • Workshops, seminars, and case-study-based teaching approach.
  • Entrepreneurship Development Cell supporting student startups and innovations.
