Kristu Jayanti College

Established in 1999, Kristu Jayanti College is one of Bangalore’s leading autonomous colleges affiliated with Bengaluru North University. The college has built a strong reputation for its Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program, combining academic rigor with real-world business learning.

Unique Features

  • Ranked among the top BBA colleges in Bangalore by multiple national surveys.
  • Focuses on innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership development.
  • Active industry collaborations and corporate interface programs.
  • Dedicated placement cell ensuring high employability and skill development.
