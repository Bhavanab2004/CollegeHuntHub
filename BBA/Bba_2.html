NMIMS Bengaluru — BBA Program

Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS) Bengaluru is one of India’s top private universities, renowned for business education and innovative learning. The BBA program prepares students for global corporate environments through strong foundations in management and technology.

Unique Features

  • Industry-integrated curriculum aligned with global business needs.
  • Strong placement support and corporate connections.
  • Modern infrastructure with smart classrooms and innovation labs.
  • Emphasis on leadership, ethics, and decision-making skills.
