St. Joseph’s College of Commerce (SJCC) — BBA Program

Founded in 1882, St. Joseph’s College of Commerce is one of Bangalore’s oldest and most reputed institutions. Its BBA program emphasizes holistic education, leadership, and ethical management through academic excellence and community service.

Unique Features

  • Autonomous institution with a strong academic heritage.
  • Focus on ethics, leadership, and social responsibility.
  • Modern curriculum aligned with industry standards.
  • Vibrant student community and extracurricular exposure.
