Ramaiah College of Arts, Science and Commerce

Established in 1994, Ramaiah College of Arts, Science and Commerce is one of Bangalore’s most respected institutions for business education. The BBA program focuses on developing entrepreneurial, analytical, and leadership skills among students to prepare them for the corporate world.

Unique Features

  • Dynamic curriculum designed to meet global business demands.
  • Dedicated placement and internship programs with reputed companies.
  • Active entrepreneurship cell and business clubs for hands-on learning.
  • Highly qualified faculty with both academic and industry experience.
