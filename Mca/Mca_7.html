BMS Institute of Technology (BMSIT) - MCA

BMS Institute of Technology is one of the prominent institutions in Bangalore known for its advanced academic facilities and strong placement records. The MCA program at BMSIT focuses on producing skilled professionals with strong programming and analytical skills.

Unique Features

  • AICTE approved and affiliated with VTU.
  • Focus on Data Science, Cloud Computing, and AI in MCA curriculum.
  • Modern computer labs and dedicated innovation centers.
  • Active placement cell with reputed recruiters like Infosys, TCS, and Wipro.
