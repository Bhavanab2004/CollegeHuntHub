New Horizon College of Engineering (NHCE) - MCA

New Horizon College of Engineering (NHCE) in Bangalore is a reputed technical institute offering a robust MCA program. The curriculum emphasizes software development, database management, and emerging technologies to prepare students for successful IT careers.

Unique Features

  • AICTE-approved MCA program with modern IT curriculum.
  • Well-equipped computer labs and software development facilities.
  • Industry-linked projects, internships, and workshops for skill enhancement.
  • Experienced faculty guiding students in academics and research projects.
