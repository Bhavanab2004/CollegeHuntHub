St. Joseph's College (Autonomous) — Bangalore

Founded in 1882, St. Joseph’s College is one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions in Bangalore. Its BCA program focuses on comprehensive computer education and strong moral values, producing well-rounded IT professionals.

Unique Features

  • Autonomous and NAAC ‘A+’ accredited institution.
  • Focus on both core computer concepts and social responsibility.
  • Advanced programming labs and career guidance programs.
  • Vibrant cultural and tech communities on campus.
