Mount Carmel College, Bangalore

Established in 1948, Mount Carmel College is a premier institution for women offering top-notch education. The BCA program emphasizes computer science fundamentals, analytics, and personal growth.

Unique Features

  • Autonomous institution affiliated with Bengaluru City University.
  • Excellent academic record with focus on leadership and innovation.
  • Special focus on IT skills, cloud computing, and data science.
  • Highly qualified faculty and supportive environment for women.
