University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE) - M.Tech

University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE), one of India’s oldest engineering institutions, offers M.Tech programs that emphasize research, innovation, and technical excellence. As a government engineering college affiliated with Bangalore University, UVCE is renowned for its academic heritage and quality education.

Unique Features

  • Established in 1917 by Sir M. Visvesvaraya — a legacy of excellence.
  • Strong research ecosystem with government-funded projects.
  • Affordable government fee structure with high-quality faculty.
  • Located in the heart of Bangalore, offering strong industry exposure.
