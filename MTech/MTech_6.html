Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering (DSCE) - M.Tech

DSCE, Bangalore offers a comprehensive M.Tech program focused on advanced engineering, research, and practical learning. The college emphasizes skill development, industry readiness, and innovative projects for postgraduate students.

Unique Features

  • Accredited M.Tech programs with modern syllabus and industry focus.
  • Advanced labs, research facilities, and innovation centers.
  • Strong industry collaborations and internship opportunities.
  • Experienced faculty guiding students in research and projects.
