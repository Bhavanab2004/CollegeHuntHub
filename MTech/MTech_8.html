Jain (Deemed-to-be University) - M.Tech

Jain University, one of Bangalore’s top private universities, offers M.Tech programs designed to nurture research, innovation, and advanced engineering skills. The programs emphasize practical application, interdisciplinary learning, and global exposure.

Unique Features

  • AICTE-approved M.Tech programs with modern curriculum and research orientation.
  • Industry-linked projects with top companies like IBM, TCS, and Infosys.
  • Centers of Excellence in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Aerospace Engineering.
  • Flexible learning options with focus on innovation and entrepreneurship.
